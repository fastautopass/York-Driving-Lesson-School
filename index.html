Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer automatic driving lessons in York?

Yes, we provide automatic driving lessons throughout York. Automatic lessons are helpful for learners who find manual gear changes challenging or prefer a simpler approach to learning.

Do you offer manual driving lessons in York?

Yes, manual driving lessons are available across York. Learning in a manual car gives you a full manual licence, which allows you to drive both manual and automatic vehicles.

How much do driving lessons cost in York?

Lesson prices vary depending on the type of lesson and whether you book individual lessons or a block. Please get in touch using the enquiry form for current pricing and availability.

Can nervous drivers book lessons?

Absolutely. We work with nervous drivers regularly and understand that everyone learns at their own pace. Lessons are calm and structured, and we can start in quieter areas to help build confidence gradually.

Do you cover local York test routes?

Yes, all lessons include practice on roads and routes that are commonly used for driving tests in York. This helps learners feel familiar and prepared on test day.

Do you offer intensive driving courses in York?

Yes, intensive driving courses are available for learners who want to make faster progress. These courses involve multiple lessons over a shorter period and can be tailored to your current skill level.

Can I book refresher driving lessons?

Yes, refresher lessons are available for anyone who passed their test but hasn't driven for a while. These lessons help rebuild confidence and cover any areas where you feel less certain.

How many driving lessons will I need?

The number of lessons varies depending on your starting point and how quickly you learn. Most learners need around 40-50 hours of lessons, though this can be more or less depending on individual progress.

Are lessons available in the evenings and weekends?

Yes, lessons are available at times that suit your schedule, including mornings, afternoons, evenings, and weekends. We do our best to work around your commitments.

What areas of York do you cover?

We provide lessons across York city centre, Acomb, Clifton Moor, Heworth, Fulford, Tang Hall, Rawcliffe, Bishopthorpe, and nearby areas. Pickup and drop-off is included.

Is the instructor DVSA approved?

Yes, all lessons are provided by a fully qualified DVSA approved driving instructor with local knowledge of York roads and test routes.

Can I have a trial lesson before booking a course?

Yes, you can book a single lesson to see if our teaching style suits you before committing to a longer course or block booking.

What happens if I need to cancel a lesson?

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Please get in touch for full details on our cancellation policy.

How do I make an enquiry?

You can use the enquiry form at the top of this page to send us your details. We'll get back to you as soon as possible to discuss availability and answer any questions you have.
